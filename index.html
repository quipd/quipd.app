02
Write six words
Use the daily word in exactly six words. Not five. Not seven. The constraint is the creativity — every single word has to earn its place.
03
The chain grows
Heart your favourites. At midnight, the winning response — the one with the most hearts — gives birth to tomorrow's word. We take the last meaningful word from it, make sure it hasn't appeared in the past year, and that becomes the next prompt. No editors. No curation. Just the community choosing what resonates. On quiet days when no one writes, we draw from a curated list of evocative words to keep the chain alive. Over weeks and months, it becomes a collective poem that no one planned but everyone wrote.
